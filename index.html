Crystal Collector

Instructions


  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game. "Number To Get"
  • There are four crystals below.
  • By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your running total to the random number.
  • You lose the game if your running total goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Gem values reset upon starting a new game.
  • GOOD LUCK
